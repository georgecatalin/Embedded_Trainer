Core idea

Spin locks are similar to mutexes in multithreading applications.

Quick analogy
Mutex -> like waiting in line and sleeping until it is your turn (OS places the thread to sleep)
Spinlock -> like checking the door's handle in a tight loop (is it available now? but now? now?)

Spinlocks waste CPU when waiting, but they avoid overhead when going to sleep and waking up.

When to use each
Lock held for a very short time -> use a Spinlock
Lock held for a long time -> use a Mutex

Tiny to small critical section -> use a Spinlock
